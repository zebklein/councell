Accordion Books

My daughter Sarah taught English in China for two years after college. She brought back several Chinese folding books with simple but exquisite paintings in each. It inspired me to make my own folding books in this style. They are made mostly of parts of watercolor paintings along with other papers, magazine cutouts, ink, rubber stamps, fabric, and calligraphy.
