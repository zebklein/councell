Ruth Tietjen Councell

Birthplace: Orange, California — January 24, 1949
Currently residing in Kalamazoo, Michigan
Website: www.councell.net

Education

  • 2009 — Certificate of Botanical Art, Morton Arboretum, Lisle, IL
  • 1998-2009 — Studied botanical art at Chicago Botanic Garden, Morton Arboretum, and NY Botanical Garden
  • 1982-1984 — Graphic Design & Illustration, UC Riverside Extension
  • 1969-1970 — Art Studies, UC Santa Barbara, College of Creative Studies
  • 1967-1969 — Art Studies, University of Redlands, CA

Employment

  • 2010-2018 — Art instructor for adults at Paul Robeson Center, workshops across NJ (Watercolor, Botanical Art, etc.)
  • 2004-2018 — Co-curated 22 exhibitions at Trinity Cathedral, Trenton
  • 2004-2008 — Co-chaired NJ Chapter of Episcopal Church and Visual Art
  • 2005-2006 — Assisted teaching at Cambridge School for children with learning differences, Pennington, NJ
  • 2004-2005 — Taught art to inner city children at Trinity Cathedral Academy, Trenton, NJ
  • 1988-1997 — Illustrated 6 children's picture books (See Attached)
  • 1980-1984 — Illustrator & designer, UC Riverside Office of Communications

  • 2010 — Trinity Cathedral, Trenton - *Comfort and Joy (Oil Paintings)*
