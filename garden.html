Garden Watercolors

The education director at the Princeton Arts Council, where I taught from 2005-2018 was also President of the Garden Club of Princeton. She arranged for me to teach a painting class in the private gardens of various of her friends and garden club members. It was called “Painting Private Paradises” and became a very popular class.
