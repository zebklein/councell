Garden Watercolors

The education director at the Princeton Arts Council, where I taught from 2005-2018 was also President of the Garden Club of Princeton. She arranged for me to teach a painting class in the private gardens of various of her friends and garden club members. It was called Painting Private Paradises. The class became very popular and successful. I also taught en plein-aire (in the out of doors) at the Trent House Museum, Morven Historical Home, and in public gardens and parks in and around Princeton.
