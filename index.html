A Statement from the artist:


  "Ever since I can remember, drawing and painting have been my passion and my joy. My art continues to evolve and change along with the different seasons of my life. It reflects time and place, and is infused with the spirit of the people around me. From 1986 to 1997, I had the pleasure of illustrating six books for children. Then, from 1997 to 2003 I immersed myself in the study of botanical illustration. After that I went into a period I like to call 'Great Big Oil Paintings for No Apparent Reason'. Now, unexpectedly, I find myself drawn back into botanical art, and at the same time, to illuminated manuscripts and letters."

"I hope you will return to this website from time to time to see what is new."
