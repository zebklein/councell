Jackets

The beautiful colors and flowing quality of Indonesian batik scarves made me want to wear them. So I pieced four of them together, and that became the first “scarf jacket”. Many more followed. I gave some away, kept some, and took some to the Kalamazoo Institute of Arts gift shop (where I originally bought the scarves!) and the jackets were sold there.
