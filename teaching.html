Teaching

Over the years I have enjoyed teaching art to children and adults in many different venues and settings: Trinity Academy in Trenton, The Cambridge School in Pennington, NJ, the Arts Council of Princeton, Princeton Day School, The Watershed Nature Center, and Grounds for Sculpture. I always learn so much when teaching.
