Teaching

Over the years I have enjoyed teaching art to children and adults in many different venues and settings: I have taught watercolor, botanical art, children's book illustration, Chinese style folding books, Illuminated Letters, and Ukrainian Easter Egg Decorating. I always learn so much when teaching.
